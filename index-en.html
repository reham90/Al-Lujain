about us

LUJAIN group started in 2009 as a modest wood factory and furnature trading company. After successfully completing more than 20 projects as wood factory in 2011 LUJAIN has vertically integrated. The group maintained an organic growth strategy with it's two business models till 2014 when the company shareholders decided to vertically integrate the all-trade fit out. With a focus on off-site manufacturing which remains the main operating model of LUJAIN group. We believe in alignment with the market unprecedented demand and the Saudi vision 2030.
